Huli Forecast is a predictive tool designed to estimate fish count in Laguna Lake based on environmental conditions. Users input rainfall, temperature, and water hyacinth proliferation data. The system first forecasts key water quality parameters, which are then used to predict the approximate fish count by area to represent the Laguna Lake. This localized approach provides a more detailed and data-driven view to support sustainable fisheries management and decision-making.
