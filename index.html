Purpose

Why it matters?

The Fish Count Calculator is a tool designed to assist researchers and marine biologists in estimating fish populations in a given area. By inputting various parameters such as the size of the area, the type of fish, and other relevant data, users can obtain an estimate of the fish count. This tool is particularly useful for conservation efforts, fisheries management, and ecological studies.
