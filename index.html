LAGUNA LAKE-CAST

Preserving Laguna Lake Through Predictive Insights

Why it matters?

Overview

Forecasting fish count allows fishing communities around Laguna Lake to anticipate changes in fish availability. With over 13,000 fishermen relying on the lake for income and Metro Manila depending on it for fish supply, being able to predict declines or increases in fish populations helps communities plan ahead, reduce losses, and maintain stable food access.
