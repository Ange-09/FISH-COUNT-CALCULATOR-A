LAGUNA LAKE-CAST

Preserving Laguna Lake Through Predictive Insights

Why it matters?

We developed Laguna LakeCast to offer predictive insights into the health of Laguna Lake. The proliferation of water hyacinth and fluctuating water quality significantly impact the lake's fish count. Our research models these relationships to support informed decision-making. This website helps preserve Laguna Lake by providing crucial data for sustainable management strategies.
